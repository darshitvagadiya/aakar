About Us

With an experience of more than three decades in trading in the sanitary ware industry, Uday Refractories Works has been committed in providing high quality and affordable sanitary wares to the Indian as well as Global market.

We at Uday Refectories Works trade into wide range of sanitary ware products including Bathroom concepts, Wash basins, Water closets, Urinal, Sink and Accessories.

Our leading brands that we work with Uday Ceramics established in 1991 and Sunline Ceramics in 1993 have their footprints in Local and International market.

The countries where it currently exports include Nepal, UAE, Nigeria, Kenya, Saudi Arabia and other Middle East and African markets.
